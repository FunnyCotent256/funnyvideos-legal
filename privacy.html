Privacy Policy

This application respects your privacy.

Here's how we handle your data:

  • When you authorize the app via TikTok (OAuth), we receive an access token and your open ID.
  • This data is used only to post videos on your behalf via TikTok's official Content Posting API.
  • No personal data is stored online or shared with any third party.
  • Your access token is stored locally on your own device and is never uploaded anywhere.
  • You can revoke access at any time from your TikTok account settings.

By using this application, you agree to this privacy policy.
